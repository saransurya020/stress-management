About Stress

  • What is stress?
    • Stress is a physical, mental, or emotional reaction to pressure from a perceived challenge or threat in life. It's a natural human response to situations that require an adjustment or a response, like meeting deadlines or dealing with problems.
  • Why students face it?
    • Students face stress from academic pressures like exams, social factors like peer pressure, and personal issues such as lack of sleep or poor time management.
  • Signs of stress
    • Common signs include headaches, fatigue, irritability, anxiety, social withdrawal, and difficulty concentrating.
